Help - Form 268a

{% blocktrans %}

  1. Select the date - this must be today's or tomorrow's date
  2. Click on "Add a planned burn"
  3. Complete the burn details
  4. Before SDO approval, all burns must be submitted at the district level and endorsed at the regional level
  5. Use the filter buttons to select your region / district
  6. To submit burn/s at the district level, tick the box/es next to the burn and select "District Submit", click on "Update" button
  7. To endorse burn/s at the regional level, tick the box/es next to the burn and select "Regional Endorsement", click on "Update" button
  8. To approve burn/s at the state level, tick the box/es next to the burn and select "State Approval", click on "Update" button
{% endblocktrans %}

Help - Form 268b

{% blocktrans %}

  1. Select the date - this must be today's or tomorrow's date
  2. Use the filter buttons to select your region / district
  3. Enter new burns using the "Add Burn" button
  4. Enter new bushfires using the "Add Bushfire" button
  5. The (r) next to a burn indicates that it was rolled over from the previous day's records i.e. it was active yesterday
  6. All previously active burns and bushfires must be updated with the status (active/inactive) and area burnt yeterday (ha or km)
  7. Before SDO can acknowledge the fire load, all burns and bushfires must be entered at the district level and endorsed at the regional level
  8. To enter burns and bushfires at the district level, tick the box/es next to the burn and select "District Entered", click on "Update" button
  9. To acknowledge burns and bushfires at the regional level, tick the box/es next to the burn and select "Regional Acknowledgement", click on "Update" button
  10. To acknowledge burns and bushfires at state level, tick the box/es next to the burn and select "State Acknowledgement", click on "Update" button
{% endblocktrans %}
